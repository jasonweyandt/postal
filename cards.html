Cards

  • Bad Day - (23) move +1
  • Bad Day - (6) move +2
  • Bad Day - (3) move +3
  • Bad Day - (2) move +4
  • Day Off- (4) move 0
  • Vacation - (3) move -3
  • Therapy - (3) move +1 OR remove a postal stamp for any personality
  • Going Postal - (3) move +3 OR if postal, give a marker stamp to any personality OR eliminate any personality with a marker stamp
  • Suspicion - (3) move -1 OR remove a marker stamp for any personality and receive a vendetta stamp
  • Work Party - (1) move -3 THEN all personalities play the top card of the draw pile until the personality that started the party plays the top card of the draw pile or until a personality moves Sam into the post office. All cards played in this way have no actions other than it's movement
  • Wrong delivery - (1) move +1 THEN chose one card from your hand to switch with a randomly chosen card from another personality's hand
  • Overtime - (1) move +1 THEN the next personality takes 2 normal turns consecutively
  • Lost Delivery - (1) move +1 THEN choose a personality, they discard their hand and redraw
  • Crisis - (1) move +7 THEN pick the personality that goes next
  • Paranoia - (1) move +5 THEN give a marker stamp to any personality, if postal do not give a marker stamp
  • Stuffed Mailbox - (1) move +2 THEN place this card in front of another personality, that personality immediately draws an extra card to their hand and may not draw up to their hand allowance until they have played all cards from their hand, and returned this card with the last card played
  • Small Talk - (1) move +1 THEN look at any personality's hand
  • New Hire - (1) move +5 OR bring back a personality that has been eliminated and remove this card from the game, it is not reshuffled in a new year
  • Rehabilitation - (1) move +3 OR remove 1 or 2 postal stamps for any personality OR remove 1 postal stamp for 2 different personalities
  • Mandated - (1) move -1 THEN randomly choose a card from a personality's hand and place it face up in front of them, they must play this card on their next turn
  • Scheming - (1) move +3 THEN give a vendetta stamp to any personality
  • New Schedule - (1) move +3 THEN switch the positions of any 2 personalities around the board

Rush Delivery Expansion

  • Running Late - (1) -1 to movement of card played
  • Cancelled Delivery - (1) +1 to movement of card played
  • Indscision - (1) randomly play a card from the current personality's hand
  • Unbalanced - (1) when a personality goes into the post office, remove a postal stamp from any personality
  • Suspicious Package - (3) cancel a played card, the personality whose card was cancelled takes another turn
  • Pick-up a Shift - (2) choose the personality that plays next
  • Switch Days - (1) take any stamp from any personality for yourself
  • Opened Package - (2) look at the top 4 cards of the draw pile, arrange them in any order, and place them back on top
  • Damaged Package - (1) take the top card from the discard pile then discard down to your hand allowance
