Postal, The Board Game



In Postal: The Board Game, everyone is a personality in Sam's head vying to be the only one left. Each day that you control Sam can bring you closer to being the sole personality, whether crazy or sane, the choice is up to you. Hang on to your sanity or go totally nuts. Eliminate other personalities with extreme prejudice. Do what you must to take control.


This is Sam. Sam has problems. Sometimes he goes to therapy but mostly he goes to work. Sam works all day delivering packages to people. Sam hates his job. Sometimes he has good days but mostly he has bad days. Sam has has multiple personalities in his head. Sam is losing his mind. Sometimes he is sane but mostly he is crazy. Sam lives one year at a time. Sam hopes this will be the last year. Sometimes he says "just one more year" but mostly he just wants it to end one way or another. Sam wants to be sane. Sam wants to be crazy. Sometimes he cares which he becomes but mostly he just wants to be one or the other.
