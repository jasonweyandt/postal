Things to Note


When cards refer to any personality it includes the personality playing the card
Sam always moves before actions unless the card say OR then Sam will not move if the action is chosen
Sam only travels around the neighborhood in one direction, + movements are clockwise and - movements are counterclockwise
Sam can never move into the post office with a - move, he stops at the first space outside of the post office when moving counterclockwise even if he hasn't moved all spaces designated by the card
Basic Strategies
Save a therapy card to play as your first turn after you move Sam into the post office
Use a Scheming card to give an already eliminated personality a vendetta stamp
If the year ends with all personalities having at least 1 postal stamp and at least 2 personalities are tied for the most postal stamps then all personalities are in a race to have the outright most postal stamps, this includes personalities that weren't involved in the tie
